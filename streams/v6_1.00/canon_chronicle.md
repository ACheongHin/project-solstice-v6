**CHAPTER ID:** 1.00 (The Wandering Inn)  
**IN-WORLD DATE:** Day 1 in Innworld (evening/night)  
**LOCATION:** The Wandering Inn (isolated hilltop structure in mountain pass region)

**Timeline Events:**
- Erin Solstice, while preparing to use the bathroom in her Earth home, is suddenly transported to a dark cave
- She encounters a sleeping Dragon; it awakens and breathes violet flame at her
- Erin evades the flame (burns her right arm and right leg) and flees the cave
- While running through rolling foothills, she is pursued by small green creatures with red eyes and sharp teeth (identified by her as "Goblins")
- Goblins slash at her legs as she flees; she manages to lose them
- Storm breaks as she approaches The Wandering Inn (abandoned for years, situated on hilltop between mountain passes)
- She seeks shelter inside as rain intensifies
- She explores the ground floor and kitchen, locates a bucket
- She ventures upstairs to the second floor, finds a moldy curtain (mistaken for a monster, briefly)
- She spends the evening cleaning the common room tables, chairs, and bar with the curtain as a rag
- As she rests, exhausted, she receives system notifications: **[Innkeeper Class Obtained!]**, **[Innkeeper Level 1!]**, **[Skill – Basic Cleaning obtained!]**, **[Skill – Basic Cooking obtained!]**

**Characters Introduced:**
- None directly (inn is empty)

**Creatures Encountered:**
- Dragon (violet-flame breathing, cave-dwelling)
- Goblins (small, green-skinned, red-eyed, sharp teeth, aggressive)
- Curtain (nearly fatal emotional threat)

**Locations:**
- Earth bathroom (point of departure)
- Unknown cave (dragon lair)
- Rolling foothills with hills, valleys, purple grass
- Mountain pass region with vast cliffs
- The Wandering Inn (common room, bar, kitchen, upstairs corridor, bedroom)

**World Mechanics Revealed:**
- Classes exist and are assigned by system (Class notification received)

- Leveling system appears operational
- No visible source of the system notifications (internal voice? divine intervention?)
